# Hosted Agents

<div class="grid grid-cols-[2fr_1fr] gap-4 mt-10">

<div>

**Hosted Agents** are AI agents managed and deployed on cloud infrastructure to perform specific business tasks autonomously.

- **Reliable Automation:** Handles customer service, lead generation, and data processing 24/7.
- **Scalability:** Unlike local setups, hosted solutions offer better reliability and can scale with business needs.
- **Strategic Implementation:** Used for managing entire sales funnels and complex scheduling without human intervention.
- **No-Code Efficiency:** Bridging the gap between complex AI tech and practical, high-ROI business applications.
